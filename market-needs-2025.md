What “the market needs” Means in Practice (for a business)
Unmet demand / weak supply: Where existing solutions are insufficient, or barriers exist.
Scalability & accessibility: Many innovations remain niche; scaling them to mainstream is a gap.
Bridging complexity: Many firms want AI, sustainability, etc., but lack capabilities — so they need tools, services, integrators, education.
Trust / regulation / safety: As capabilities grow, regulatory and ethical constraints tighten; there's need for infrastructure (governance, auditing, compliance).
Interoperability & standards: New systems must play nicely across platforms, devices, geographies.
Contextualization / localization: Global trends exist, but local adaptation matters — culture, regulation, infrastructure differ.
